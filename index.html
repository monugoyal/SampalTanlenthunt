About Our Vision

At MSA, we believe in discovering and nurturing talent. We offer a number of scholarship tests including MSA Talent Hunt Exam (kHOJ-3) which offers students a chance to win up to 100% scholarship and cash scholarship awards. KHOJ-3 is conducted for Class VI, VII, VIII, IX, X, xI,XII studying students. It is a scholarship examination that aims to help the deserving students towards achieving their goal of becoming engineers and doctors by providing them with scholarship and giving them an opportunity to succeed. In Synch with our vision to become the leading educational group that works towards the overall advancement of the society by enabling the talented minds to achieve what they desire and deserve to, Target is conducting Talent Hunt Scholarship Examination in every year. Thereby we invite all the young talented minds to come and explore this long lasting legacy as the start to their first right step towards a rewarding and successful career path. Best Wishes by Team Target.




Rules & Instruction

The vision of Apex Public School is to provide comprehensive educational experiences that will empower our students to become lifelong learners and productive, informed, responsible citizens who are able to meet the challenges of a rapidly changing society. We also aim to bring forth the unique abilities of each child so that they can realise their true potential. We aspire to educate young minds and foster ethical, social and moral values through holistic learning to bloom into responsible global citizens.
